# 🐧 Ice King Quiz - Adventure Picker

Discover your perfect adventure with the Ice King-inspired personality quiz!

## Features

✨ **Smart Recommendations** - Get personalized trip suggestions based on your preferences

🎨 **Beautiful UI** - Gradient backgrounds, smooth animations, and responsive design

🧊 **Ice King Personality** - Random commentary as you interact with the quiz

📱 **Mobile Friendly** - Works great on all devices

📧 **Email Integration** - Send your results via email

## How It Works

1. **Answer the Questions**
   - Select your vibe (Chill, Balanced, MAX CHAOS)
   - Choose your travel style (Road trip, Stay put, Mix it up)
   - Pick activities you're interested in
   - List your must-haves
   - Select your budget range

2. **Get Recommendations**
   - Click "SUMMON TRIPS" to generate personalized trip suggestions
   - 6 unique trip options based on your selections

3. **Rank Your Choices**
   - Sort the recommended trips (1st, 2nd, 3rd choice)
   - Click "SEND MY RANKINGS" to email your results
